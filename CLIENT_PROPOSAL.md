## What We're Building

A desktop application that runs on your computer (like Microsoft Word or Excel) designed specifically for mortgage brokers. It helps you:

1. **Record client meetings** with automatic transcription
2. **Store and organise client documents** securely
3. **Track client financial details** (income, assets, liabilities)
4. **Match clients to the best bank** based on their situation
5. **Generate professional proposals** quickly

## How It Works (The Simple Version)

### For You (The Broker)
- Install the app on your computer
- When you meet with a client (in person or via video call), click "Record"
- The app captures the conversation and creates a transcript
- Your client emails you payslips and documents? The app pulls them from Outlook automatically
- All information is organised in one place
- The app suggests which banks might be suitable based on your client's situation

### For Your Clients
- They experience a more professional, organised process
- Their sensitive information is handled securely
- You can generate detailed proposals quickly

## Data Security & Privacy

This is the most important part. Here's how we protect your clients' information:

### Local-First Design
**Everything is stored on YOUR computer first.** Not in the cloud. Not on our servers. On your machine.

Think of it like this:
- **Traditional cloud software** = Storing files in someone else's filing cabinet
- **Our approach** = Storing files in your own locked filing cabinet at home

### Encryption
All data is encrypted using military-grade encryption (AES-256). Even if someone got physical access to your computer, they couldn't read the data without your password.

### Compliance
We build with Australian compliance requirements in mind:
- **MFAA/FBAA Code of Conduct** - Industry standards for mortgage brokers
- **ASIC RG 209** - Responsible lending obligations
- **Privacy Act 1988** - Australian privacy principles
- **7-year retention** - Required record keeping period

### Audit Trail
Every action is logged:
- Who accessed what data and when
- What documents were viewed or downloaded
- Any changes made to client records

This protects both you and your clients.

## Storage Options

### Option 1: Local Only (Recommended for MVP)
**How it works:** Everything stays on your computer

**Pros:**
- Maximum security - no internet connection needed
- Fast performance
- No ongoing server costs
- You're in complete control

**Cons:**
- Data doesn't sync between multiple computers automatically
- You need your own backup system (we recommend external drive or encrypted cloud backup)

**Cost:** $0 ongoing

---

### Option 2: Self-Hosted Server (Future Option)
**How it works:** Your data lives on a server you control (could be in your office or a private cloud)

**Pros:**
- Multiple brokers can access the same data
- Centralised backup
- Still under your control

**Cons:**
- Requires server maintenance
- Higher setup cost
- Needs IT management

**Estimated Cost:**
- Setup: $2,000 - $5,000
- Monthly: $100 - $300 (server hosting)

---

### Option 3: Hybrid (Future Option)
**How it works:** Sensitive data stays local, non-sensitive data syncs to shared services

**Pros:**
- Best of both worlds
- Client details stay private
- Bank policy updates shared across team

**Cons:**
- More complex setup
- Requires careful configuration

**Estimated Cost:**
- Setup: $3,000 - $7,000
- Monthly: $50 - $150
